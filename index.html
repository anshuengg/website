About

Who we are

  • Founded by Mr. Ravi Dhokare
  • We providing services since 2022
  • Our Core services are Profile machining & cutting, Facing, Threading, Milling, Turning.

Established with a commitment to pushing the boundaries of possibility, Anshu CNC Machining was founded by Mr. Ravi Dhokare, who recognized the transformative potential of CNC technology. Our founders' combined experience and vision laid the foundation for a company that seamlessly marries craftsmanship with cutting-edge techniques.

Thank you for considering Anshu CNC Machining as your partner in innovation. We invite you to explore our website, learn more about our services, and get in touch with us to discuss how we can turn your ideas into reality. Let's embark on this journey together, where precision meets craftsmanship and innovation knows no bounds.
